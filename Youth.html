​GUIDELINES FOR PROFESSIONAL PRACTICE FOR ACTORS

  • Full attendance is required:  if you cannot attend due to illness etc you must email your facilitator to let them know.  If you do not attend a total of 3 sessions we reserve the right to give your place to someone else from our waiting list.

  • Please be punctual to all sessions: arrive 15 minutes early.  We would ask the person who is picking the young person up to be on time.  Facilitators cannot leave until all young people have left.

  • Full commitment during workshops is required.

  • We ask that you wear appropriate clothing to all workshops ie. Track bottoms, trainers and loose tops.  Leave jewellery etc at home.
